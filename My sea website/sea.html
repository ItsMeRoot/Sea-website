Deep Sea Fish!

Aliens! I mean- deep sea creatures, pretty lovely. (You can click on a picture to jump to the animal)

Like you've read a thousand times before in any website or video, life in the depth of the ocean is very difficult. So the adaptions and evolution these organisms have achieved is quite phenomenal.



Here are some not-so-cool lame ones. So enjoy these totally boring facts.







Ghost Fish:


Ghost fish are scaless fish. For some reason in every deep sea video, ghost fish are the wimps that nobody cares about.



Yeti Crab:


Yeti crabs live near hydrothermal vents, that's it, that's what I know about them.



Vampire Squids:


Cool squid bois that eat marine snow, which is fish poop and dead bodies.
